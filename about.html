Denise M. Spencer

Biography

I am a New Orleans native whose career in public relations, marketing and communications is rooted in the largest city to claim the sights, sounds and effects of Lake Michigan.

I provide B-to-C as well as B-to-B freelance services in PR, traditional marketing, digital marketing, copy editing, communications and social media content for Global 360 Marketing.

Most recently I secured coverage for Chicago Public Schools’ YWLCS on stations ABC, NBC, FOX, WBBM Radio, iHeart Radio, as well as print coverage, for its Girl Power STEM panel.

Although public relations and marketing are currently the most dominant presence in my life, I am a journalist at heart. In fact, my professional career began as a writer for a New Orleans newspaper, followed by a reporting position with an Army newspaper in Wildflecken, West Germany, where I earned the US Army Certificate in Journalism.

Public relations and marketing are in my blood. But my true loves are, in this order: Family, books, biking, news and politics.


And yes ... COFFEE!
